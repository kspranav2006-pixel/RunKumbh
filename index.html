About the Event

The Story Behind
the Run

The RunKumbh Marathon is an initiative by the NCC cadets of RVITM College to promote fitness, discipline, and community spirit among students and the general public.

This year's marathon blends the energy of the summer sun with the freshness of the monsoon — a celebration of endurance, spirit, and togetherness.

All proceeds support NCC activities and social causes.
